Scientific Means of Forgery Detection

Objectives

To prepare trainees through technical training and advancement in the field of questioned documents examination. 2/ to assist trainees in acquiring the necessary knowledge’s and skills in the discovery and proof of facts for avoidance crimes of forgery and counterfeit

Training Methods

Presentations, Videos, Practice using some tools, Discussions and Group work

Course Content
  • 1. HANDWRITING: Basic rules governing the individuality of handwriting, and how handwriting is Identified?
  • 2. SIGNATURES: It’s Types, Importance’s and Indications of genuine and forged one, In addition to modern simulated techniques
  • 3. CHEQUES: Tests for ‘Raised ‘cheques and drafts and techniques on how to avoid loss from bad cheques
  • 4. BANKNOTES: Description of banknotes and elements of genuine Banknotes and techniques on how to detect counterfeit
  • 5. BANKNOTE SECURITY FEATURES: Technical study of: a. S. Sudan Banknotes “SSP “ b. USA Banknotes “USD” c. EURO Banknotes. d. UK pound Banknotes
  • 6. Measures to be taken when detecting a counterfeit
Target Group

Financial Institutions staff & Law Practitioners
